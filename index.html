Professor: TEAV CHHUNNAN(លោកសាស្រ្តចារ្យ​ ទាវ ឈុនណន)

Teav Chhun Nan Mr.Chhunnan used to work as Dean College of Science and Technology over 18 years universities, He got involved in Electronics and Telecommunications, Computer Science and Information Technology. Background Educations 1994: Studied at Royal Phnom Penh University Department Mathematics. 2002: Engineer of Electronics and Telecommunications, Department of Electronics and Telecommunications Hanoi University of Technology. 2004: Master of Sciences Electronics and Telecommunications, Department of Electronics and Telecommunications Hanoi University of Technology
